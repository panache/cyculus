#VirtualRealityMountainBiking

Get ready for the most exciting cardio experience ever created.

About

In today's hectic world keeping active and healthy is increasingly difficult. With minimal time to engage in fun and exciting sports that drive the motivation to stay fit, many people resort to the confined and boring gym.

With the advent of virtual technology, people have been able to experience more and more things without all the hassle.

Our goal is to dive into the growing world of virtual reality by creating a mountain biking simulator; focusing on delivering an immersive, entertaining experience while providing a proper cardio workout.

By pairing haptic feedback through an actuated stationary bicycle with the Oculus Rift, a revolutionary stereoscopic virtual reality headset, we are able to provide a dynamic exercise experience unlike anything before.

Dynamic environments and routines experienced in real mountain biking provide a unique motivation to push beyond one's limits. The Cyculus provides this drive without the hassle, time commitment, or dangers of actually doing it.
